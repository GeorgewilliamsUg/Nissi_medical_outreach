ABOUT US

Who We Are

Nissi Medical Outreach is a Christ-centered community health initiative committed to strengthening healthcare access in underserved Ugandan communities. We exist to serve with excellence, dignity, and love, reflecting the hope of Christ by addressing practical medical needs and promoting long-term wellbeing.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate medical outreach, preventive care, and health education to underserved communities across Uganda. We equip families with essential health knowledge and support vulnerable groups through services that promote dignity and long-term wellness.

Our Vision

A Uganda where every community has access to quality healthcare and health education. We envision communities empowered with knowledge and resources to maintain their health and wellbeing through Christ-centered compassionate care.

Our Impact

Through medical missions, community health education, and partnerships with local healthcare workers, we have touched hundreds of lives across Uganda. Our work focuses on preventive care, health literacy, and building sustainable healthcare practices in rural and underserved communities.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Baale Emmanuel

Founder & Medical Director

Dr. Baale Emmanuel is a dedicated physician and visionary leader who founded Nissi Medical Outreach with a heart for serving Uganda's underserved communities. With extensive experience in community health and a deep commitment to Christ-centered care, Dr. Emmanuel leads our mission to bring hope, healing, and dignity to those who need it most.

His passion for preventive healthcare and community empowerment drives our organization's approach to sustainable health solutions across Uganda.
