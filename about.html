ABOUT US

Who We Are

Nissi Medical Outreach is a registered Community Based Organisation committed to strengthening preventive health, youth empowerment, and reproductive health education across Uganda. We are dedicated to providing exceptional medical services and health education to underserved communities.

Our Mission

To promote health and dignity by equipping families and young people with knowledge, preventive practices, and access to essential services. We focus on preventive health education, medical outreaches, youth training, and reproductive health support.

Our Impact

Through community visits, school partnerships, church collaborations, and joint activities with local health workers, we've helped families improve health information, hygiene, and access to care.

Our Vision

A world where quality healthcare and compassionate service are accessible to all. We believe in bridging gaps in medical accessibility and strengthening community wellness through dedicated outreach and education.
