About Us

Serving with excellence, dignity, and love to restore hope in our communities.

WHO WE ARE

Restoring Hope Through Healthcare

Nissi Medical Outreach is a Christ-centered community health initiative committed to strengthening healthcare access in underserved Ugandan communities.

We exist to serve with excellence, dignity, and love, reflecting the hope of Christ by addressing practical medical needs and promoting long-term wellbeing. Our team works tirelessly to bridge the gap between vulnerable populations and essential medical services.

Our Purpose

Driven by Compassion

Our Mission

To provide compassionate medical outreach, preventive care, and health education to underserved communities across Uganda. We equip families with essential health knowledge.

Serving with Dignity

Our Vision

A Uganda where every community has access to quality healthcare. We envision communities empowered with knowledge to maintain their health and wellbeing.

OUR HISTORY

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2018, Nissi Medical Outreach began as a small group of medical students and volunteers visiting a single village. Witnessing the overwhelming need for basic healthcare, the team committed to returning regularly.

Over the years, what started as a small mobile clinic has grown into a comprehensive organization serving thousands annually across multiple districts, driven by the same passion to serve the vulnerable.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Nissi Medical Outreach?

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds! Whether you are a medical professional, student, or have other skills to offer, you can join our team. Please visit our Volunteer page to fill out an interest form.

Where are your medical missions located?

Our missions primarily target underserved rural and peri-urban communities across Uganda. We focus on areas with limited access to government health facilities, including districts like Mukono, Gulu, and Masaka.

How are donations used?

100% of public donations go directly towards our programs. This includes purchasing medicines, medical supplies, organizing outreach camps, and supporting health education initiatives. We maintain strict financial accountability.

Do you accept medical supply donations?

Yes! We gratefully accept donations of unexpired medicines, medical equipment, and supplies. Please contact us directly at nissimedicaloutreach@gmail.com to coordinate a drop-off or shipment.
