OUR MISSION

We provide Christ-centered medical outreach and compassionate healthcare to underserved communities in Uganda.

Nissi Medical Outreach runs regular medical clinics, basic clinical care, and preventive health education across rural and peri-urban districts. We work with local health workers to deliver practical, dignified care to people who lack reliable access to services.
