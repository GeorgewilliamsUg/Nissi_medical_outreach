Help Us Build a Community Clinic

While our mobile clinics are effective, the need for a permanent presence in Mukono District is growing. We are raising funds to construct a small community health center that will operate 24/7.

This facility will provide maternity care, emergency first aid, and ongoing treatment for chronic conditions.
